# Vermont Technical Centers Interactive Map

An interactive web application that maps all 17 Career & Technical Education (CTE) centers across Vermont, displaying key statistics, programs offered, and educational outcomes.

![Vermont Tech Centers](https://img.shields.io/badge/Centers-17-green) ![Students](https://img.shields.io/badge/Students-5%2C450+-blue) ![Cost](https://img.shields.io/badge/Cost-FREE-brightgreen)

## Features

### 📍 Interactive Map
- Visual map of all 17 Vermont technical centers
- Click any center for detailed information
- Filter centers by program category

### 📊 Key Statistics
- **17 Technical Centers** across Vermont
- **FREE** for all high school students
- **93% graduation rate** (vs. 80% national average)
- **90%** of graduates go directly to work or college
- **37%** of graduates earn college credit while in high school

### 🎓 Program Information

Each center profile includes:
- Location and contact information
- Number of students served
- Graduation rates
- Complete list of programs offered
- Specializations (Healthcare, Skilled Trades, Technology, Culinary Arts, Automotive)

### 🔍 Filtering

Filter centers by program area:
- Healthcare
- Skilled Trades
- Technology/Digital Media
- Culinary Arts
- Automotive/Transportation

## About Vermont CTE Centers

Vermont's Career and Technical Education centers provide tuition-free vocational training to high school students beginning in their sophomore year. These centers offer hands-on learning in:

- **Healthcare** - Nursing, Medical Assisting, Pharmacy Tech
- **Skilled Trades** - Welding, HVAC, Building Trades, Electrical
- **Advanced Manufacturing** - Machining, Mechatronics, Engineering
- **Technology** - Digital Media, IT, Business Technology
- **Culinary Arts** - Professional cooking and food service
- **Automotive** - Auto technology, diesel mechanics
- **Agriculture** - Farming, natural resources
- **Early Childhood Education**
- **Cosmetology**

## Why CTE Matters

### Higher Success Rates
- CTE students have a **93% graduation rate** compared to the national average of 80%
- **90%** transition directly to employment or college after graduation
- Students gain real-world skills and industry certifications

### Economic Impact
- Addresses Vermont's skilled labor shortage
- Provides workforce-ready graduates
- Over **40%** of CTE students have Individualized Education Plans (IEPs), showing inclusive education success

### Financial Benefits
- **Completely FREE** to students
- No application fees
- Opportunity to earn college credits (37% of students)
- Industry certifications included
